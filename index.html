WHAT IS FOOLS FEST?

🌿

The Greenhouse

A DIY artist collective of young professionals based in Seattle, WA that books, promotes, and manages local shows in support of the underground and up-and-coming Seattle scene.
🎪

Fool's Fest

An independent rock music festival held indoors at the Punk Rock Flea Market in Capitol Hill, hosting 7 of Seattle's best up-and-coming local rock, punk, psyche, and hardcore acts.

🤡

The Vibe

Following April Fool's Day—think jesters, clowns, playing cards, clown makeup galore. Art vendors, skating pipes, flash poetry, curated thrift, and so much more.
